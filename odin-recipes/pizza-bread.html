Garlic bread pizza

Quick and easy to make Pizza, good for a snack and meal.

  • Bread: Preferably Sub rolls but Hotdog buns work as well
  • Canned tomato: A can of tomato sauce & a can of El Pato
  • Seasonings: Oregano,
  • cheese: cottage cheese or mozzarella, or both.
  • Pepperoni: Your choice of Pepperoni
