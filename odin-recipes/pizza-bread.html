Garlic bread pizza

Quick and easy to make Pizza, good for a snack and meal.

    Ingredients

  • Bread: Preferably hoagie bread but Hotdog buns work as well
  • Canned tomato: A can of tomato sauce & a can of El Pato
  • Seasonings: Oregano, Garlic salt, & black pepper.
  • cheese: cottage cheese or mozzarella, or both.(Shredded)
  • Pepperoni: Your choice of Pepperoni

    Steps

  1. Prepare the bread: cut the hoagie bread's in half.
  2. Make the sauce: pour one can of tomato sauce & El Pato,
    add 2 1/2 tsp of Oregano, Garlic Salt & Black Pepper.
    (There is the option to let the sauce sit to Marinate with the Seasonings.)
  3. Assemble the Pizza: the Bread layered with sauce, Cheese, and finally Pepperoni.
  4. Bake the Pizza: Place tray of Pizza's in the preheated oven(400 degrees F)
    for 10 minutes. Remove tray and serve.
