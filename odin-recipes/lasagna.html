Baked lasagna pasta with mozzarella

A lasagna recipe with a meaty tomato sauce and delicious cheese filling

    Ingredients

  • Meat: 1 pound of Ground Meat(1/2 pound ground pork, 1/2 pound lean ground beef).
  • Onion: Diced Onion cooked till translucent.
  • Canned tomatoes: Can of tomato sauce & crushed tomatoes.
  • Fresh herbs: Chop two tablespoons of Parsley & crush one clove of Garlic.
  • Sugar: A pinch of Sugar.
  • Spices & seasonings: Dried basil, Dried Oregano, Salt, & Black Pepper.
  • Noodles: Uncooked noodles.
  • Cheese: Cottage cheese & Parmesan, shredded mozzarella.
  • Eggs: 2 Eggs (Makes cheese layer extra creamy)

    Steps

  1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent.
    Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna.
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven(375 degrees F) for about half an hour.
    Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown. (total Duration: 50 min)
